The Beauty Of The SolarSystem

Diving into space, we explore the diverse shapes, sizes, and components of planets in our solar system. From the rocky terrains of Mercury to Jupiter's massive gaseous expanse, each planet offers a unique glimpse into the cosmos

How Planetary Data Helps Us Understand Space

Planetary data,gathered from robotic missions and telescopes,enhances our understanding of space by revealing the composition,formation and evolution of celestial bodies.

The Earth

The Earth revolves around the Sun in an elliptical orbit, taking approx. 365.25 days to complete one full rotation . This orbit causes seasons and year cycles. Earth also rotates on its axis causing day and night
